Water Wars

A simple game built using the HTML5 canvas and vanilla javascript as a mini project for our coursework as well as to deepen the understandings of javascript functionality and object oriented approach.

Fend of enemy ships and alien creatures in this planet of unknown origins filled with water and unknown alien tech with your trusty shaceship and collect power up orbs along the way to defeat the alien colony and its motherships.

Objective: Score 80+ in 40secs to win.
